SMC enhanced digital reach by giving each dealer a personalized, easy-to-manage website. These responsive sites reflected dealer branding, upheld SMC’s identity, and showcased unique products, promotions, and contact information.

Brief

We built a CMS-enabled website platform that allows SMC dealers to showcase their products, company profile, and brand identity. Each dealer can manage their content and products independently through a dedicated admin panel. A centralized SMC admin panel allows management of the master catalogue and seamless communication with dealers.

Goals

  • Deliver a responsive and branded website template for each SMC dealer
  • Enable dealers to easily manage their own content and product listings
  • Provide a seamless experience for customer queries via forms and WhatsApp
  • Centralize product management via the SMC admin panel
  • Ensure mobile optimization, fast loading, and SEO readiness
